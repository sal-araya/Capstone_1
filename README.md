#Capstone 1 - Sales Territory
Project Overview- This project analyzes data for a selected territory using of SQL and Excel. It was to a identify trends, performance, and provide data recommendations for improving sales outcomes.
Tools used in here are SQL(Analysis) and Excel (Visualization)
Analysis Performed:
  -Calculated revune and identified the time period of sales data
  -Monthly revenue trends
  -Comparing territory with its region
  -Ranked store performance within the territory
A small number of stores generate the majority of revenue 
Certain states and locations consistently outperform others
Recommendation- Focus sales efforts on improving lower performing stores while analyzing and replicating strategies from top-performing locations. Additionally, product categories to maximize revenue growth in the next quarter.
